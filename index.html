Can I freeze my membership?

Many fitness centers offer the option to freeze or suspend your membership for a designated period, such as for travel, illness, or other personal reasons. It's advisable to contact your gym's membership services or check their terms and conditions for detailed information on how to initiate a membership freeze and any associated fees or criteria.

What is the class cancellation policy?

The class cancellation policy typically requires notifying the gym or class provider within a specified timeframe, often 24 hours in advance, to avoid any cancellation fees or penalties. Be sure to review the specific policy of your fitness center for accurate details and guidelines.

What kinds of classes are right for me?

The ideal classes for you depend on your fitness level, interests, and goals. Consider options like strength training, cardio, or group classes for a balanced approach. Consult with a fitness expert to help you choose the classes that best suit your needs
